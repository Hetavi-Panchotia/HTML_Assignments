Aamir Khan says his cameo in Rajinikanth's Coolie was 'a mistake'

Now, in an interview, the clipping of which has been doing the rounds on X, Aamir himself has admitted that his special appearance in the Lokesh Kanagaraj-directed action thriller was "a mistake" and said that the part was "badly written". "I accepted the cameo for Rajini saab. To be honest, I still can't figure out what my character was meant to do," said Aamir. He added: "It felt like I just walked in, said a line or two, and disappeared. There was no real purpose, no thought behind it. It was badly written."
